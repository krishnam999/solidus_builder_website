Our Story

Founded in 2005, Solidus Builders began as a small residential construction company with a big vision: to redefine quality construction through craftsmanship, integrity, and client-focused service.

What started as a team of three passionate builders has grown into a full-service construction firm with over 50 skilled professionals, serving both residential and commercial clients across the region.

Our journey has been marked by milestones of growth, innovation, and an unwavering commitment to our founding principles. Every project we undertake carries this legacy forward.
